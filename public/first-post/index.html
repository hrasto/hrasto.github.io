First Post!

December 20, 2023

After magically 1 missing last week’s NAACL submission deadline, I decided it is about time to think about my past (and future) research. Somehow I feel (and mainly hope), that the long-lasting (relative to the length of my PhD contract) stroke of cynicism, a part of my quarter-life crisis, is beginning to temper. So, here I am, building what I always thought could not be built due to lack of content: my personal website. To my great surprise, when I listed and counted my publications, including a poster which I contributed to in my bachelor’s thesis and some papers I am not proud of, the total amount turned out to be 5! I thought that is not bad actually.

The plan is to, occasionally, post here about my publications, research-related observations, career progress, or just general thoughts. Let’s pray it won’t be too cringey and regrettable. You will not find any emojis here. See you!
